## Billing Cycle

- **Hourly Billing**: Pay for resources only when they're running
- **Monthly**: Dedicated servers and reserved instances
- **Annual**: Save up to 20% with yearly plans

## Understanding Your Invoice

Each invoice includes:
- **Compute**: Instance usage charges
- **Storage**: Block storage and snapshots
- **Networking**: Load balancers and bandwidth
- **Other**: Reserved IPs, additional services
