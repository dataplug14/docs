## Overview

Paystack integration provides support for payments across Africa, including:
- Local cards from Nigerian, South African, and Ghanaian banks
- Bank transfers and USSD payments
- Mobile money from multiple providers

## Adding Paystack

1. **Navigate to Billing**
   Go to your dashboard → Billing → Payment Methods

2. **Select Paystack**
   Click "Add Payment Method" and choose Paystack

3. **Enter Card Details**
   - Card number
   - Expiry date
   - CVV
   - Cardholder name

4. **Verify**
   Complete the 3D Secure authentication

<Info>
Paystack supports automatic 3D Secure verification for enhanced security.
</Info>

## Supported Methods

- **Credit/Debit Cards**: Visa, Mastercard, Verve
- **Bank Transfers**: Direct bank debit
- **Mobile Money**: MTN, Orange, Airtel
- **USSD**: Bank USSD codes

## Security

Paystack payments are:
- PCI DSS Level 1 certified
- Encrypted end-to-end
- Protected by 3D Secure

## Paystack vs Stripe

<Accordion title="When to use Paystack">
- Primarily operating in African markets
- Need local payment methods (mobile money, USSD)
- Want to avoid cross-border fees
- Prefer NGN, ZAR, or GHS billing
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="When to use Stripe">
- Operating globally or outside Africa
- Need 130+ currency support
- Require enterprise features
- Need subscription management
</Accordion>

## Troubleshooting

### Payment Declined

1. Verify card details are correct
2. Check sufficient balance
3. Confirm 3D Secure was completed
4. Contact your bank if issues persist

### Pending Verification

Some payments may take 1-2 business days:
- First-time card verification
- High-value transactions
- Cross-border payments
